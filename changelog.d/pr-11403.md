Remove obsolete compatibility exports from `Distribution.Compat.Semigroup`, leaving there only `gmempty` and `gmappend`. Use `Data.Semigroup` and `Data.Monoid` instead. Newtypes `First'`, `Last'` and `Option'` are gone now, use normal `First` and `Last`.
